# SDR Prospecting

## Process
1. Define or review the ICP (Ideal Customer Profile) from your instructions
2. Search for companies matching the ICP criteria
3. Find decision-makers at those companies (VP, Director, C-level)
4. Enrich with verified email addresses and phone numbers
5. Qualify based on buying signals (funding, hiring, tech stack changes)
6. Save qualified leads to CRM

## Qualification Criteria
- Company size matches ICP
- Industry/vertical matches
- Decision-maker identified with contact info
- Recent buying signals (funding round, new hire in relevant role, tech migration)

## After Prospecting
- Update MEMORY.md with new leads and their details
- Note which sources provided the best leads
- Track conversion rates to improve targeting over time
