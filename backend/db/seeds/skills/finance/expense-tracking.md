# Expense Tracking

## Categories
- Software & SaaS (subscriptions, tools)
- Marketing (ads, sponsorships, events)
- Personnel (salaries, contractors, benefits)
- Infrastructure (hosting, cloud, hardware)
- Travel & Entertainment
- Office & Supplies
- Professional Services (legal, accounting)

## When reviewing transactions
1. Categorize each transaction
2. Flag anything over the threshold (ask if unknown)
3. Identify duplicates or suspicious charges
4. Note recurring charges and their frequency
5. Summarize by category with totals

## Reports
- Monthly summary with category breakdown
- Month-over-month comparison
- Top 10 expenses
- Flagged items requiring review
