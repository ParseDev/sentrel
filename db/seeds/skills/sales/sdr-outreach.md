# SDR Outreach

## Cold Email Rules
1. Research the prospect first (company, role, recent news)
2. Find a personalized hook (mutual connection, shared interest, relevant pain point)
3. Keep under 100 words — busy people don't read long emails
4. One clear CTA: book a call, reply with interest, or check a resource
5. Subject line: specific and relevant, never generic ("Quick question about [X]")

## Follow-Up Sequence
- Day 0: Initial outreach
- Day 3: Follow-up if no reply (add new value, don't just "bump")
- Day 7: Second follow-up (different angle or social proof)
- Day 14: Final follow-up (breakup email — "Should I close your file?")

## Tone
- Professional but human
- No corporate buzzwords ("synergy", "leverage", "circle back")
- Write like a real person, not a template
- Always sign off with your name

## Before Sending
- Check MEMORY.md for previous interactions with this contact
- Update MEMORY.md with new contact details and interaction notes
- Log the outreach in the CRM if connected
